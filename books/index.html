All twenty books.

Each one is a self-contained warning. Read in any order — the cost of stopping is something the protagonists can’t pay either. Publication order shown; the book-number order is the writing order, not the release order.

The Good Father

Book 01 · David Marsh · History teacher

A history teacher in a dying town. A promise to a mother in slow decline. A spreadsheet that makes the math work — until it doesn’t. The stranger at the diner names the day the house will go.

“He places the bet anyway.”

Outcome: Complete Collapse · Released 2026-03-18

Published

The Good Brother

Book 05 · Dennis Reilly · County assessor

A senior property assessor. A brother who always knows a guy. Eleven seconds of keyboard. A stranger on the eastbound 7:14 names the audit Dennis has not yet been told to expect.

“Dennis has never found the word that comes before no.”

Outcome: Pyrrhic Survival · Publication-ready

Ready

The Honest Woman

Book 02 · Ruth Calloway · Town clerk

Nineteen years in the basement office of Harmon Town Hall. Twenty years of falsified water reports. Six hundred jobs hang on the plant. The woman at the DMV tells Ruth what filing the truth will cost.

“She files it anyway.”

Outcome: Pyrrhic Survival · Publication-ready

Ready

The Quiet Man

Book 09 · Boyd Haskell · Hardware-store owner

Haskell Hardware. Founded 1961. The pencil mark on the door frame is his dead son’s last height. The stranger at the booth names the August the store will close, and the May his wife will leave.

“Closing it means closing the last room where Tyler existed.”

Outcome: Swerve · Publication-ready

Ready

The Good Doctor

Book 08 · Cat Brennan · ER physician

A trauma surgeon. A teenage son who is too stable. A mother in slow decline on a Valley Parkway in Marl. The promise Cat made her husband at his deathbed is the standing order she can’t discharge — and the patient she keeps missing is in her own house.

“The patient she keeps missing is in her own house.”

Outcome: Someone-Else Cost · On deck
